Bestmates Eatery

The Best Indian Restaurant in Windsor

Established in 2024, Bestmates Eatery is a cozy Indian restaurant located in Windsor, Canada. We specialize in authentic Indian snacks and street food. From crispy samosas and spicy chaat to flavorful pakoras and tangy pani puri, our menu brings you a delicious variety of small bites that capture the vibrant essence of India’s culinary heritage. With a warm ambiance and friendly service, Bestmates Eatery is the perfect spot to satisfy your cravings for Indian street food.

Restaurant Specials

Discover our unique fusion specials, like Butter Chicken Poutine. Our signature "Naanzas" combine the soft texture of naan bread with the bold flavors of Indian sauces, fresh vegetables, and mouthwatering toppings—bringing you a one-of-a-kind taste experience.
