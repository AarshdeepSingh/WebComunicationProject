Bestmates Eatery

Best Indian Restaurant in Windsor

Bestmates Eatery, opened in 2024, is a cozy Indian restaurant in Windsor, Canada, specializing in authentic Indian snacks and street food. From crispy samosas and spicy chaat to flavorful pakoras and tangy pani puri, the eatery offers a delicious variety of small bites that capture the essence of India’s vibrant culinary culture. With a warm ambiance and friendly service, it’s the perfect spot to satisfy your cravings for Indian snacks.

Restaurant Specials

Bestmates Eatery offers unique specialties like Butter Chicken Poutine. Their signature Naanzas replace pizza crust with soft naan bread, topped with Indian sauces, fresh veggies, and flavorful toppings, delivering a delicious fusion experience.
